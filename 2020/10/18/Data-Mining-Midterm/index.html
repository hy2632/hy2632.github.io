Radius basis function kernel(RBF) is defined as

Condition 2: exp⁡(−12(x−y)⊤Σ−1(x−y))=exp⁡(−∣∣x−y∣∣22σ2)\exp(-\frac{1}{2}(x-y)^{\top}\Sigma^{-1}(x-y)) = \exp\big( -\frac{||x-y||^2}{2\sigma^2}\big)exp(−21​(x−y)⊤Σ−1(x−y))=exp(−2σ2∣∣x−y∣∣2​)

Let z=x−y∈Rdz = x-y \in \mathbb{R}^{d}z=x−y∈Rd,

Combine results from the previous 2 parts

From Part 2,

det⁡(Σ)=∏i=1dλi=(σ2)d\det({\Sigma}) = \prod_{i=1}^d{\lambda_i} = (\sigma^2)^d det(Σ)=i=1∏d​λi​=(σ2)d
